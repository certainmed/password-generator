## Design Context

### Users
General public — everyday people who need a quick, trustworthy way to generate strong passwords. They may not be technical; they want confidence that the tool works, without having to think about it.

### Brand Personality
Calm, trustworthy, precise. Three words: **clear, reliable, quiet**.

### Aesthetic Direction
Clean and minimal. Warm off-white light mode, deep warm dark mode via system preference. Pastel accent colors (user-selectable via theme switcher) applied only to interactive elements — not the page background. No heavy shadows, no glassmorphism, no gradient text.

Typography: Space Grotesk (headings), DM Sans (body), JetBrains Mono (password output and history).

Layout: centered single column, max-width 520px, generous whitespace, sections separated by subtle borders rather than cards-within-cards.

Anti-references: avoid the "neon on dark" look, avoid heavy drop shadows on everything, avoid Inter/Roboto defaults.

### Design Principles
1. **Password output is the hero** — it sits at the top, large and legible.
2. **Controls are secondary** — grouped cleanly below with minimal visual noise.
3. **System-native by default** — light/dark via `prefers-color-scheme`, no forced mode.
4. **Accent color as the only variable** — theme switcher changes one accent color, not the whole page.
5. **Every element earns its place** — no decorative shadows, no redundant labels.
